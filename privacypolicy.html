Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024/10/06

At Destinex, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use Destinex, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name: To identify you.
  • Email Address: For communication purposes.
  • Phone Number: For customer service and booking confirmations.
  • Payment Information: Including credit card details, for processing transactions.
  • Travel Preferences: Such as preferred destinations and activities.
  • Account Information: Username, password, and other account details.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

We may also collect non-personal information such as:

  • Browser Type: To improve our website's compatibility.
  • Device Information: For optimizing our mobile experience.
  • IP Address: For security and analytical purposes.
  • Cookies: To enhance your browsing experience and track usage patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide Services

  • Booking and Reservations: To process and confirm your bookings.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance.
  • Personalization: To tailor your experience based on your preferences.
